Volunteer Experience

COVID-19 Donation Drive

2020

Donation Drive Head

Headed a donation drive for tricycle drivers and nurses in our province. Our team were able to raise 145,000 php. This collection helped us to provide food packs to 430 families, and tokens and ear savers to 105 medical professionals.

Other COVID-19 Related Volunteer Work

2020

Volunteer

Volunteered as one of the editors of Flatten The Curve PH. My primary duty was to turn COVID-19 related information into infographics that can be easily understood by the general public.

Volunteered to make face shields for medical professionals for one week.

Volunteered to be one of the organizers of the donation drive called: Hello, Covid Goodbye, a donation drive for providing personal protective equipment (PPE) to Department of Health – Region 3, and food relief to the Aeta Community of Sitio Pidpid in Pampanga. My task was to contact PPE suppliers and to solicit to the organization’s alumni and to some organizations.
