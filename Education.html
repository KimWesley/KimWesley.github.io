Education

High School

Philippine Science High School - Cagayan Valley Campus

From 2011-2015, I studied at Philippine Science High School Cagayan Valley Campus. My fascination in Engineering started here as I found our Robotics class really exciting and challenging. In that class, we were asked to simulate simple to complex engineering problems in the real world. There, I became the founding president of the robtics club.

Tertiary Education

University of the Philippines Diliman

Since 2015, I am studying Mechanical Engineering at University of the Philippines Diliman. Here, I studied Thermodynamics, Machine Design, Powerplant Engineering, Airconditioning, and etc.

Student-Exchange Program

Hokkaido University

In 2019, I studied here as an exchange student for 5 months. Here, I finally was able to take courses that are not offered in my home university. Those subjects are Law and Economics, Robotics, Medical Device and Biomaterials, Japanese Management, and Agriculture in Hokkaido

I would say that this semester is one of the higlights of my college life as I was able to learn courses that I really like while immersing in different communities in Japan.
